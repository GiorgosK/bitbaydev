Privacy Statement

We believe that personal data of visitors of our website should be treated with the utmost care. When processing personal data BitBay therefore acts in accordance with the Dutch Data Protection Act and other privacy legislation.

Processing personal data
As part of our service, BitBay records data. This is the case when you ask questions or post comments on our website. This means that we ask you to provide us personal information or that you share this on your own initiative. In those cases, your data will be processed only to determine your identity. Your information will not be provided to third parties unless BitBay is obliged to do so by law, by court order or when you have consented to this.

Click behaviour
BitBay stores general visiting data of this website. This data is used to improve the quality of this website.

Cookies
Some parts of this website may contain cookies. Cookies are small pieces of information (files) that a website stores on your computer. The website instructs your browser to do so. Cookies are not used to obtain nor save personal information. By adjusting the settings of your browser you can change this procedure. In these cases, it is possible that you may not have access to certain areas of our website.

Our website contains components which are protected by intellectual property rights.

Other sites
You will find links to other websites on this website. BitBay is not responsible for the way these parties treat your information. We recommend that you read the privacy statement of the site you visit.

Security
We have taken appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against loss or any form of unlawful processing. Persons that act on behalf of BitBay and have access to your data are bound by secrecy.

Changes
BitBay preserves the right to make changes to this privacy statement. We therefore recommend that you periodically consult this privacy statement.

More information
If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please let us know. Also, if you find inaccuracies or missing information on the website, we would like to hear from you. You can contact us via email.
