Take a Closer Look at BitBay

BitBay Team
Dec 21 2016
Share on Facebook Share on Twitter Share on linkedin Share on Google Plus

Everywhere you look there are crypto currencies out there asking you to put your trust in their ideas and grand promises before writing a single line of code.

BitBay has more to offer, including a functional market client with smart contracts and a fully functioning wallet, in addition to well thought out, practical and realistic ideas for the future.

BitBay’s plans for the upcoming year are exciting, including the release of user friendly contract templates, and currency pegging based on a dependable and realistic approach.

The word is starting to get out. The price of BAY is up in the last few months and people are talking. The conversation over in the Bitcointalk thread is lively. You will start to see more activity in the forums, blogs, and on Slack as we make a concerted effort to keep people informed and tell people how to get involved. We want you to join us.
